# Project Brief Builder

Build a brief from messy project materials by reorganizing evidence around business questions.

## Workflow
1. Inventory available materials and identify file types, dates, owners, and likely business meaning.
2. Ask what the briefing audience and decision are if unclear.
3. Organize findings by business questions, not chronology: current status, key decisions, blockers, risks, next actions.
4. Attach source references to important conclusions. Do not invent missing facts.
5. List contradictions and missing information separately.
6. Save deliverables in a `成果` folder: `汇报摘要.md`, `PPT大纲.md`, `演讲提纲.md`, `补充材料清单.md`, and `矛盾与缺口.md`.
7. Add durable briefing rules to `AGENTS.md` when the pattern should be reused.
